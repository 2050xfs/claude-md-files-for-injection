The Collection

"Art as memory. Culture as inheritance."

Poppy's Collection is a privately held archive of rare and culturally significant works collected over a lifetime by Keith "Poppy" Williams.

Now stewarded by his daughter, the collection is being opened to collectors, institutions, and the public in new ways — honoring a legacy built piece by piece, decade by decade.

This is not a gallery. It is not a shop. It is a living archive — and you are invited in.
